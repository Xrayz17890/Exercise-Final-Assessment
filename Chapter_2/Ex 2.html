Overview

FIFA is a football simulation video game series developed by EA Sports. It allows players to control real teams, compete in tournaments, and play online matches.

Official FIFA Website

Gameplay

FIFA offers realistic football gameplay with advanced mechanics, skill moves, and tactical control. Players can play Career Mode, Ultimate Team, and online matches.
