Pizza Pepperoni

Pizza Pepperoni is a delicious Italian-style pizza made with crispy dough, tomato sauce, melted cheese, and spicy pepperoni slices.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Olive oil
  • Oregano
  • Salt and pepper

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly.
  4. Add mozzarella cheese.
  5. Place pepperoni slices on top.
  6. Add oregano and olive oil.
  7. Bake for 12–15 minutes.
  8. Cut and serve hot.
